Frequently Asked Questions

When are HOA assessments due?

Assessments are due on April 15 each year.

How much is the assessment?

As of April 2025, the assessment is $125 per lot (subject to change).

How can I pay my assessment?

Mail a check payable to Salem Estates Homeowners Association to:
SEHOA
4640 Thompson Road
Springfield, Illinois 62711
*Late payments incur a 10% penalty every 30 days.

What community events does the HOA host?

We host an annual end-of-summer garage sale (typically the first weekend in August) and, in partnership with Salem Estates West, a neighborhood Easter egg hunt in the spring.

What's the difference between Salem Estates and Salem Estates West?

Salem Estates West is our sister HOA located directly to our west. It covers the section of Longfellow Drive between Bradfordton Road and Maggie Drive, and all properties on Maggie Drive or west of it. Salem Estates includes Gray Court, Blake Court, Whitman Drive, Kilmer Court, Casey Drive, Abigail Drive, Shalegh Avenue, Fitzgerald Drive, and Thompson Road.

When are the annual meetings?

The annual meeting is scheduled each year. Homeowners receive advance notice with the date, time, and location.

How do I verify if assessments/dues are up-to-date when buying or selling a home?

Please email our board at salemestateshoa@gmail.com with complete details, and we will respond promptly.
